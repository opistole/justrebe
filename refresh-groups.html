A few quick answers.

What's the difference between the cohort and a 1:1?

The cohort ($300) is a five-week group experience with the full ReBe team and a small group of fellow participants. It's deeper, longer, and the group itself is part of the work. A 1:1 ($197) is a single one-on-one hour with the confidant of your choice — better if you want one focused conversation in private.

Is this therapy?

No. ReBe ReFresh is a guided group experience and private coaching. It's not therapy or clinical care. If you're in acute crisis, please reach out to a therapist, your doctor, or 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline).

Is this religious?

ReBe is faith-friendly, not religious. Some of our confidants come from Christian backgrounds; others do not. Spiritual framing is welcome, secular framing is welcome, and neither is required to participate fully.

Are the sessions recorded?

No. Nothing is recorded — these are the times you set aside to actually be there. That's part of the practice.
