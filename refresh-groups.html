Why ReBe exists

Somewhere between a therapy room and a classroom.

Between a survivor's story and a child's silence, we found the same wound — and we built ReBe to heal it. Most of us were never taught how to use our imagination. We were handed fear loops, inherited worry, and survival patterns, and ran them so long they started to feel like us.

But the story isn't finished. You cannot heal what you've never been helped to understand.

ReBe is a guided, root-cause path from isolation into real community — picturing safety, envisioning freedom, rehearsing a future worth living. No performance. No pressure.

Questions

Things people actually ask.

Is this therapy or coaching?

No. ReBe ReFresh is a guided process for inner work — faith-friendly, with company. It's not therapy, coaching, or pastoral counseling. If you're in acute crisis, please reach out to a therapist, your doctor, or 988 first.

What's included in the cohort?

Six weekly live sessions with Elizabeth and the ReBe team, in a small group. Not recorded — they're the times you set aside to actually be there. $497 total. The first group starts June 23 at 11 AM Eastern.

What's the difference between the cohort and a private session?

The cohort ($497) is six weeks of guided group work. A standalone private session ($197) is a single 1:1 with the confidant of your choice — book one anytime, on its own.

How does payment and scheduling work?

Payment is handled securely at checkout after you submit the form. The first cohort starts June 23 at 11 AM Eastern. For a private session, your confidant reaches out within 48 hours to schedule directly.

Can my group or organization enroll together?

Yes — email refresh@justrebe.com and we'll help you set it up.
