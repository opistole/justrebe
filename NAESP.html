What is ReBe Ed

An ACE-informed wellbeing platform built for the youngest learners.

ReBe Ed brings the science of self-awareness, emotional regulation, and identity-formation into the rhythms of the school day — not as another curriculum to bolt on, but as five-minute Health Boosts that fit into what your teachers are already doing.

The result: students who can name what they feel, classrooms that move from reactive to responsive, and a principal who finally has data to show the school board.
