1. Program description

ReBe Workshop & Program Notifications is an SMS messaging program operated by ReBe (justrebe.com). The program sends transactional and reminder text messages to participants who have voluntarily registered for our live workshops, 5-week cohort programs, or 1:1 sessions and have explicitly opted in to receive SMS messages.

Messages may include:

  • Registration confirmation
  • Reminders before scheduled events (typically one day before and one hour before)
  • Links to join scheduled events (such as Zoom meeting links)
  • Brief follow-up messages after sessions, where appropriate

This is not a marketing or promotional SMS program. All messages are tied to events you have registered for. We do not send promotional or advertising content via SMS.

2. How to opt in

You opt in to receive SMS messages from ReBe by actively checking a dedicated SMS consent checkbox at the time of registration on justrebe.com. The checkbox is separate from any other consent (such as email marketing) and is never pre-selected.

Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of registration. You may register for any ReBe program without opting in to SMS — you will still receive email communications related to your registration.

3. Message frequency

Message frequency varies depending on the events you have registered for. Typically, you can expect 2–4 messages around each event. Frequency may be higher during the week of a multi-week cohort program.

4. Message and data rates

Message and data rates may apply per your mobile carrier's plan. ReBe does not charge you for SMS messages, but your carrier may charge standard messaging rates per your contract with them. Please consult your mobile carrier for specific rate information.

5. How to opt out

You can opt out of SMS messages from ReBe at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After you reply STOP:

  • You will receive one final confirmation message acknowledging your opt-out.
  • No further SMS messages will be sent to your number for this program.
  • Your email communications and event registration are not affected.

If you want to opt back in later, you can re-register on justrebe.com and check the SMS consent box again.

6. How to get help

Reply HELP to any SMS message from ReBe to receive an automated response with contact information.

For all other questions, contact support at:
