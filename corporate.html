How It Works

Three phases.
Lasting transformation.

Phase 1
Awareness

Transformation begins with awareness. Through the ReBe app, individuals gain insight into how Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACEs), trauma, culture, and life experiences can shape behavior, stress responses, and decision-making patterns. By recognizing these influences in a safe, science-informed way, participants begin to understand the "why" behind how they show up at work and in life, opening the door for meaningful change.

The EPICS Framework

Emotional · Physical · Intellectual · Cultural · Spiritual

ReBe addresses the five dimensions of human wholeness — the root of sustainable transformation.

E — Emotional

We help employees recognize the emotional patterns that shape how they respond to stress, conflict, and pressure. With greater self-awareness and resilience, individuals communicate better and build healthier workplace relationships.

P — Physical

We help employees understand how stress and life experiences impact energy, focus, and overall health. By learning practical strategies for wellbeing, they can sustain the physical energy needed to perform and thrive.

I — Intellectual

We help employees gain insight into how experiences shape thinking, decision-making, and problem-solving. This awareness strengthens clarity, creativity, and confidence when navigating challenges at work.

C — Cultural

We help organizations build cultures of trust, respect, and stronger human connection. When people feel valued and understood, collaboration improves and teams perform at a higher level.

S — Spiritual

We help individuals reconnect with purpose, meaning, and the deeper motivation behind their work. When employees feel aligned with their values, engagement, fulfillment, and long-term commitment grow.
