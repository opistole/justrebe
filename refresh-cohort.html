The 5-week cohort

Five weeks. One small group. Your way back to who you are.

Join Elizabeth Good (M.A. Clinical Psychology) and the full ReBe team for five live Tuesday sessions of guided inner work — anchored in EPICS (Emotional, Physical, Intellectual, Cultural, Spiritual) and the story-as-genre framework.

This isn't therapy. It isn't coaching in the standard sense. It's a five-week guided experience in a small group of adults doing the same work alongside you — small enough to be known, large enough to be honest.

Five weeks of weekly Tuesday sessions with Elizabeth and the ReBe team, plus multiple optional bonus group sessions led by each of the seven confidants. You can participate openly or, if you'd prefer, anonymously by name and turned-off camera.

Whether you're working through burnout, anxiety, grief, loneliness, or simply not feeling like yourself this season — the work meets you where you are. Limited seats. First come, first served.

Questions

Things people actually ask.

Is this therapy or coaching?

No. ReBe ReFresh is a guided process for inner work — faith-friendly, with company. It's not therapy, coaching, or pastoral counseling. If you're in acute crisis, please reach out to a therapist, your doctor, or 988 first.

What's included in the cohort?

Five main weekly sessions with Elizabeth and the ReBe team, in a small group — plus multiple optional bonus group sessions led by each of the 7 confidants. Not recorded — they're the times you set aside to actually be there. $300 total. The first group starts June 23. You can pick either 11 AM or 8 PM Eastern when you enroll.

What's the difference between the cohort and a private session?

The cohort ($300) is five weeks of guided group work. A standalone private session ($197) is a single 1:1 with the confidant of your choice — book one anytime, on its own.

How does payment and scheduling work?

Payment is handled securely at checkout after you submit the form. The first cohort starts June 23 — you pick either 11 AM or 8 PM Eastern when you enroll. For a private session, your confidant reaches out within 48 hours to schedule directly.

Can my group or organization enroll together?

Yes — email refresh@justrebe.com and we'll help you set it up.

Jean Park

Relational Healing & Family Therapist
