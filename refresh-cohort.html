About the cohort

What this is.

A five-week guided group experience for adults doing inner work — built on a decades-proven framework, anchored in EPICS (Emotional, Physical, Intellectual, Cultural, Spiritual). Small enough to be known, large enough to be honest.

This isn't therapy or coaching in the standard sense. It's a structured five-week practice in a small group of people doing the same work alongside you. Weekly live sessions with the ReBe team, plus optional bonus group sessions led individually by each of the seven confidants throughout the cohort.

Whether you're working through burnout, anxiety, grief, loneliness, or simply not feeling like yourself this season — the work meets you where you are. Limited seats. First come, first served.

What you'll get

  • Five weekly live Zoom sessions (60 min each) with the ReBe team
  • Multiple optional bonus group sessions led individually by each of the 7 confidants throughout the 5 weeks
  • Live Q&A in every session — bring your specific questions
  • A small cohort — small enough to be known, large enough to be honest
  • Anchored in EPICS — Emotional, Physical, Intellectual, Cultural, Spiritual
  • A faith-friendly approach — secular framing welcome, spiritual framing welcome, neither required
  • Tools, language, and practices you can take into your life immediately
  • Not recorded — these are the times you set aside to actually be there
