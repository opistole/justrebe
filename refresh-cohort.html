About the cohort

What this is.

A five-week guided group experience for adults doing inner work — built on a decades-proven framework, anchored in EPICS (Emotional, Physical, Intellectual, Cultural, Spiritual). Small enough to be known, large enough to be honest.

This isn't therapy or coaching in the standard sense. It's a structured five-week practice in a small group of people doing the same work alongside you. Weekly live sessions with Elizabeth Good, plus 7 optional bonus sessions led by some of the ReBe ReFresh confidants.

Whether you're working through burnout, anxiety, grief, loneliness, or simply not feeling like yourself this season — the work meets you where you are. Limited seats. First come, first served.

What you'll get

  • Five weekly live Zoom sessions (60 min each) with the ReBe ReFresh team
  • 7 bonus sessions led by some of our confidants
  • A small cohort — small enough to be known, large enough to be honest
  • Anchored in EPICS — Emotional, Physical, Intellectual, Cultural, Spiritual
  • Tools, language, and practices you can take into your life immediately
