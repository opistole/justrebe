The Classroom Crisis

The Challenge in Today’s Schools

Educational outcomes are at historic lows and schools are facing overlapping challenges that affect both students and educators. Every educator brings their own life experience into the room. So does every student.

“Classrooms aren’t just academic spaces—they’re emotional ecosystems.”
Educators are burning out at historic rates
Students are struggling with self-regulation, motivation, literacy and overall well-being—emotional, physical, intellectual, cultural, and social health
Administrators are managing behavior more than academic outcomes

When educators and students carry unseen stress, it shows up in burnout, behavior, and disengagement. Collectively, these challenges constitute a drastic crisis in which students lack adequate literacy, life skills, and wellness support to thrive, while educators are also contending with unaddressed adverse childhood experiences (ACEs), burnout and increasing attrition rates.

The EPICS Framework

Emotional · Physical · Intellectual · Cultural · Social

Interactive Health Boosts and Literacy LABS™ for Every Child’s Educational Journey

ReBe addresses the five pathways of student wholeness—the root of sustainable learning and growth. ReBe’s EPICS are 7–10 minute Health Boosts for K–12 students, organized into five EPICS pathways with a total of 25 boosts per grade.

E — Emotional

Helping students recognize and regulate their emotions for resilience and stronger relationships.

P — Physical

Building habits of movement, nutrition, and self-care that fuel focus and well-being.

I — Intellectual

Strengthening critical thinking, executive function, and motivation for deeper learning.

C — Cultural

Building cultures of civility, respect, and open-mindedness in every classroom.

S — Social

Developing communication, empathy, and collaboration for healthy relationships and community.

Literacy LABS™

Learning Applied By Students

ReBe’s Literacy LABS™ (Learning Applied By Students) provides intentional spaced practice posturing a culture of inquiry and rigor. There are five Literacy LABS™ with four Strategic Stations at each of the LABS™.
