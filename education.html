The EPICS Framework

Emotional · Physical · Intellectual · Cultural · Social

ReBe addresses the five dimensions of student wholeness — the root of sustainable learning and growth.

E — Emotional

We help students recognize and understand their emotions so they can respond to challenges in healthier ways. Emotional awareness builds resilience, confidence, and stronger relationships with teachers and peers.

P — Physical

We help students understand the connection between physical wellbeing, energy, and learning. Developing healthy habits equips students to focus, participate, and succeed in the classroom.

I — Intellectual

We help students strengthen critical thinking, curiosity, and problem-solving skills. As students gain confidence in how they think and learn, they become more engaged, motivated, and prepared for success.

C — Cultural

We help schools build cultures of respect, civility, and understanding. Fostering empathy and appreciation for different perspectives makes classrooms safer environments where every student can thrive.

S — Social

We help students develop the social skills needed to build healthy friendships and positive community. Students learn communication, cooperation, and empathy — growing into confident contributors.
