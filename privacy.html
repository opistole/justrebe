1. Information we collect

When you register for a workshop, cohort, or 1:1 session, we collect:

  • Name — first and last name
  • Email address
  • Mobile phone number — only if you provide it
  • SMS consent status — whether you opted in to text messages
  • Email marketing consent status — whether you opted in to occasional emails
  • How you heard about us — optional, for attribution
  • Timestamp, IP address, and browser user-agent — when you submit SMS consent, recorded as part of our compliance audit trail

We do not collect Social Security numbers, payment card numbers (payments are processed by our third-party payment provider — we never see or store full card details), or any sensitive personal information beyond what you voluntarily share with us in registration forms or sessions.

2. How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Register you for the workshop, cohort, or session you signed up for
  • Send you the Zoom link and reminders before your event
  • Send confirmation emails and post-event follow-ups
  • Communicate with you about other ReBe programs (only if you opted in to marketing emails)
  • Maintain a record of your participation across ReBe programs
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

3. SMS messaging terms

This section governs all SMS (text) messages sent by ReBe through Twilio.

Opt-in

You opt in to receive SMS messages from ReBe by actively checking a dedicated SMS consent checkbox during registration on our website. The checkbox is separate from any other consent (such as email marketing) and is never pre-selected. Consent is not a condition of registration — you may register without opting in to SMS.

Types of messages you'll receive

  • Confirmation of your registration
  • Reminders before workshops, cohort sessions, or 1:1 sessions (typically one day before and one hour before)
  • The Zoom link to join your scheduled event
  • Brief follow-up after your session, where appropriate

Frequency

Message frequency varies based on the events you've registered for. Typically you can expect 2–4 messages around each event. We do not send promotional SMS — all messages are transactional and related to events you've registered for.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply per your mobile carrier's plan. ReBe does not charge you for SMS messages, but your carrier may.

Opt-out

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After you reply STOP, we will send one final confirmation message and no further SMS will be sent. Opting out of SMS does not unsubscribe you from email communications or remove you from our event registration.

Help

Reply HELP to any SMS for assistance, or contact us at hello@justrebe.com.

Carriers

SMS messages are delivered through major US mobile carriers. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy of mobile information

The mobile information collected from SMS consent (including phone numbers and opt-in records) will not be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All other information categories follow the policies described elsewhere in this document.

4. Email communications

When you register, we may send you transactional emails related to your registration (confirmation, Zoom link, reminders, post-event materials). If you check the optional marketing consent box, we may also send occasional emails about upcoming ReBe programs, workshops, and cohorts.

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any marketing email. Unsubscribing does not remove you from transactional emails about events you've registered for.

5. Cookies and analytics

Our website uses essential cookies required for the site to function. We may also use limited analytics (such as page view counts) to understand how visitors use the site. We do not currently use third-party advertising cookies or behavioral tracking pixels.

6. Third-party services

We use trusted third-party services to operate ReBe. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — hosts our database where registrations and contact information are stored. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Twilio — sends SMS messages on our behalf. Twilio Privacy Policy
  • Kit (formerly ConvertKit) — sends email communications. Kit Privacy Policy
  • Zoom — hosts live workshops and sessions. Zoom Privacy Policy
  • Vercel — hosts our website. Vercel Privacy Policy

7. Data retention

We retain your registration and contact information for as long as you remain a participant in ReBe programs, plus a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping and legal compliance. SMS opt-in audit records are retained for at least four years to satisfy A2P 10DLC and carrier compliance requirements.

If you would like us to delete your information, contact us at hello@justrebe.com and we will honor your request unless we are required by law to retain it.

8. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for SMS or marketing emails at any time
  • Object to or restrict certain uses of your information

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@justrebe.com. If you are a California or EU resident, additional rights may apply under CCPA or GDPR — contact us for details.

9. Children's privacy

ReBe is designed for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will make a reasonable effort to notify registered participants by email.
