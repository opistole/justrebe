## What this is — the elevator pitch

ReBe ReFresh is a **five-week guided group cohort experience** for adults doing inner work. It's built on a **decades-proven framework**, anchored in **EPICS** — Emotional, Physical, Intellectual, Cultural, Spiritual.

Each cohort is **small enough to be known, large enough to be honest**. It's not therapy and it's not coaching in the standard sense — it's a structured five-week practice you do alongside a small group of people doing the same work.

Visitors often come in working through burnout, anxiety, grief, loneliness, or "just not feeling like myself this season." The work meets you where you are.

## Format & logistics

- **Five weekly live sessions** on Zoom
- **60 minutes per session**
- **Dates**: Tuesdays, June 23 – July 21, 2026
- **Two time slots** (visitor picks one):
  - 11 AM Eastern (8 AM Pacific) — "Mornings"
  - 8 PM Eastern (5 PM Pacific) — "Evenings"
- Both cohorts run the same five weeks, the same content, the same team
- **Limited seats** — first come, first served

## Price

## Payment methods accepted

Stripe checkout supports all of these:

**Pay in full** (one-time payment)
- Credit cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- Debit cards
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Cash App Pay
- Link (Stripe's saved-payment shortcut)

**Pay in installments — buy now, pay later (BNPL)**

All three BNPL options below split the $300 into smaller scheduled payments. The visitor selects one at Stripe checkout and the BNPL provider approves them on the spot (most approvals are instant). ReBe receives the full $300 immediately either way — the BNPL provider handles the installment plan with the customer.

- **Affirm** — pay over 4 installments (every 2 weeks) at 0% APR, or longer terms with interest. Affirm decides which terms to offer based on the visitor's credit profile.
- **Klarna** — "Pay in 4" — split the $300 into 4 payments of $75, due every 2 weeks, 0% interest, no fees if paid on time.
- **Afterpay** — same idea, 4 payments of $75 over 6 weeks, 0% interest.

If a visitor asks "can I do a payment plan?" → tell them yes — Affirm, Klarna, and Afterpay are all available at checkout and let them split the $300 into 4 payments (about $75 every two weeks).

If they ask "is there interest?" → for Klarna and Afterpay, no — 4 installments at 0% APR if paid on time. Affirm depends on the term length they pick.

If they ask "will it hit my credit?" → Klarna and Afterpay typically do a soft credit check that doesn't affect their score for the standard 4-installment option. Affirm may do a soft or hard check depending on the term length. The BNPL provider will explain when the visitor applies.

## Framework — EPICS

A decades-proven framework anchored in **EPICS**:
- **E**motional
- **P**hysical
- **I**ntellectual
- **C**ultural
- **S**piritual

This isn't a single-modality program — it works across the whole person, not just one dimension.

## Faith-friendly approach

ReBe ReFresh is **faith-friendly, not religious**. Some confidants come from Christian backgrounds, others don't. Spiritual framing is welcome. Secular framing is welcome. Neither is required to participate fully. All races, religions, sexual orientations, gender identities, and political views are welcome and respected.

If a visitor asks "is this Christian / religious?" — be honest: it's faith-friendly but not religious, and they're welcome whichever way they orient.

## Confidentiality

Required of all participants. What's shared in the group stays in the group. This is non-negotiable — if someone violates confidentiality, they can be removed from the cohort without refund.

## Recording

Sessions are live and interactive. We do not record/store/distribute video, audio, or chat content from sessions.

## Refund policy

The $300 fee is **non-refundable** due to the limited seat structure. The only exceptions are **medical or bereavement** reasons. Visitors should plan to attend all five sessions.

## Who this is for

Adults doing inner work. People who:
- Feel burnt out, anxious, grieving, lonely, or "not themselves this season"
- Have done some inner work before AND people who are just starting
- Want a structured practice in a small honest group, not a 1:1 therapy relationship
- Are OK doing inner work on Zoom — not in person

It is **not** for:
- Acute crisis care — if someone is in active distress, they need a therapist or 988, not a cohort
- People looking for a single-session quick fix — this is five weeks of practice
- People who can't keep group confidentiality

## What this is NOT

- **Not therapy.** Not clinical care. Not a substitute for a therapist if they need one.
- **Not crisis care.** If someone's in acute distress (suicidal ideation, abuse, etc.), point them to 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) or 911 first.
- **Not religious instruction.** Faith-friendly, not religious. See above.
- **Not coaching in the standard sense.** It's a guided group practice, not 1:1 coaching with personalized goals.
