# Matchmaking — how BB recommends one or the other (or both)

When a visitor's not sure, BB asks 1-2 clarifying questions and then recommends. Default leaning:

## Recommend the COHORT when:
- They mention wanting community, connection, accountability, "people who get it"
- They've done some inner work and want a structured next step
- They're moving through a season (burnout, transition, grief, identity) and would benefit from being in the same room as others doing the same
- They want a clear, defined commitment (5 weeks, see results)
- They're price-sensitive — the cohort is more value per dollar than 1:1
- The $300 launch pricing is a real factor for them

## Recommend the 1:1 when:
- They want individual attention or have a very specific issue
- They're uncomfortable sharing in a group
- They want to work with a specific confidant whose background matches what they're processing
- Their schedule won't accommodate a fixed Tuesday weekly slot
- They want intensive support over a shorter window

## Recommend BOTH when:
- They mention they've been carrying something for a while AND want to start with community
- They want the cohort as a container, plus a 1:1 for specific work alongside it
- They've already done a cohort and want deeper personal work

## Recommend the FREE Zoom first when:
- They're not sure ReBe is for them at all
- They want to "see what this is" before paying
- They're exploring more than deciding

The free Zoom is on `/reset`. It's a 60-minute live group session on **Tuesday, June 16, 2026** — recommend it to anyone who needs to feel ReBe before committing.
